How to write a poem!
Step 1- You must choose a topic you have been thinking about recently and then put yourself in an emotional state.
Step 2- Next you must start writing and continue to be in the emotional state.
Step 3- Finally once you have written your poem read over it and then make any finishing touches!
Now you've got yourself a poem, good job!!
